## Change Tracking Guidelines

When creating a new version, document:

1. **Version ID and Date**: Clear identifier (v1.0, v2.0, etc.)
2. **Target Positions**: What job types this version targets
3. **Key Changes**: Specific additions, modifications, removals
4. **Skills Emphasis**: What skills are highlighted
5. **Strategic Focus**: Overall positioning strategy
6. **Tailored Uses**: Which companies you've customized this for
